About Softinn

Softinn is a hotel-tech startup based in Malacca, Malaysia. The mission is to “Make I.T easy for hoteliers.” Softinn provides cloud‑based solutions to help boutique hotels, resorts, and BnBs streamline operations and increase direct bookings — without the need for complex IT infrastructure.

Key Products 🎖
  • Property Management System (PMS) – Manages daily front‑desk operations for small to mid-size hotels.
  • Booking Engine – A commission-free tool for direct hotel bookings with local payment gateway integration.
  • Hotel Kiosk – Enables 24/7 self check-in/out and walk-in bookings directly at the hotel.
  • Channel Manager – Synchronizes room availability and rates with OTAs such as Booking.com, Agoda, and Airbnb.
  • Website CMS – A simple content management system for hoteliers to manage their own hotel websites.
Why Hoteliers Choose Softinn ✈️
  • Cloud-Based Simplicity – No server setup required. Softinn was among the first boutique-hotel providers to integrate with Google Hotel Booking Link.
  • Trusted by Many – Used by more than 1,200 hotels across Southeast Asia.
  • Recognized Solutions – Recipient of the APICTA 2022 Merit Award in the Tourism & Hospitality category.

Founded in 2013 by JeeShen Lee and Ken Lee, Softinn envisions connecting 100 subsystems to increase hotelier productivity throughout the Asia Pacific region.
